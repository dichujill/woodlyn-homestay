About Us

Welcome to WOODLYN

Woodlyn HomeStay and Boating Munroe is a peaceful riverside retreat located in the heart of Munroe Island, Kerala. Our homestay is a traditional Kerala-style home with a Diamond House classification, offering a perfect blend of comfort, nature, and culture. We provide cozy rooms, homemade Kerala food, and relaxing boating experiences through the backwaters. Whether you're here to unwind, explore, or experience village life, Woodlyn is your home away from home.

Our Services

Explore Our Services

Rooms

Cozy, clean, and comfortable rooms with traditional Kerala charm — perfect for a peaceful stay by the riverside.

Food

Savor authentic Kerala meals, freshly prepared with local ingredients. We serve delicious home-cooked dishes, including seafood specialties like Karimeen (Pearl Spot) and traditional curries — a true backwater culinary experience.

Boating

Experience the beauty of Munroe Island with peaceful Shikara boat rides and traditional canoeing through narrow canals — a perfect way to explore the backwaters up close.

GAMES

Fun for all ages! Enjoy indoor games like carrom, darts, and board games, plus outdoor activities like soccer and badminton. Evenings come alive with music and light entertainment, making your stay truly memorable for both kids and adults.

Event & Party

Celebrate your special moments at Woodlyn! We offer a dedicated space for engagements, birthdays, small weddings, and other private gatherings — all in a peaceful riverside setting with full privacy guaranteed.
